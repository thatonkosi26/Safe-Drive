About SafeDrive

SafeDrive is a smart ride safety app designed to make your journeys in South Africa safer and more reliable. By combining real-time tracking, AI-powered recommendations, and safety alerts, SafeDrive ensures you stay informed and protected throughout your ride. Whether you're a passenger or driver, SafeDrive helps you make safer choices, plan better routes, and enjoy peace of mind every time you travel.
