About SafeDrive

SafeDrive is a smart ride safety app designed to make your journeys in South Africa safer and more reliable. By combining real-time tracking, AI-powered recommendations, and safety alerts, SafeDrive ensures you stay informed and protected throughout your ride. Whether you're a passenger or driver, SafeDrive helps you make safer choices, plan better routes, and enjoy peace of mind every time you travel.

Features

SOS / Panic Button

Enables users to send an immediate distress alert with one tap. Sends live GPS location and optional real-time media (audio/video) to pre-selected emergency contacts.

Incident Reporting

Enables fast, structured reporting of threats or violent behavior. Supports anonymous submissions and optional media attachments (photos/audio).

Hotspot Map

Visual display of recent incidents on an interactive map (color-coded risk levels). Helps users avoid unsafe areas by alerting them when they approach known hotspots.

Trusted Contacts Alert System

Lets users pre-select a group of trusted emergency contacts. Automatically sends detailed alerts (with location, status) to these contacts in emergencies.
